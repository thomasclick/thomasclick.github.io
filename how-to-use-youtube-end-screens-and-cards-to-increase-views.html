What Are YouTube End Screens and Cards?

YouTube End Screens

An End Screen is a customizable screen that appears during the last 5-20 seconds of your video. You can use it to add clickable elements that promote other videos, playlists, your channel's subscribe button, or even external websites (for members of the YouTube Partner Program). It's your final, direct call-to-action to the viewer.

YouTube Cards

Cards are interactive, pop-up notifications that you can place at specific timestamps within your video. They are less intrusive than end screens and can be used to promote a video, playlist, or channel. When a viewer clicks the small 'i' icon, the card expands to show the full information. They are perfect for providing contextually relevant links while the video is still playing.

Step-by-Step Guide: Adding YouTube End Screens

  1. Navigate to YouTube Studio: Log in to your YouTube account and go to the YouTube Studio.
  2. Select a Video: Click on 'Content' from the left-hand menu and choose the video you want to edit.
  3. Open the Editor: Click on the 'Editor' option from the left menu for that video.
  4. Add End Screen: Select 'End screen' from the editor timeline. You can apply a template, import an end screen from another video, or add elements manually.
  5. Customize Elements: Add up to four elements, such as 'Video' (best for viewer, most recent, or a specific video), 'Playlist', and 'Subscribe'.
  6. Adjust Timing: Drag the elements on the timeline to set when they appear and disappear within the last 20 seconds of your video.
  7. Save Changes: Preview your end screen and click 'Save'.

Step-by-Step Guide: Adding YouTube Cards

  1. Go to the Video Editor: Follow steps 1-3 from the End Screen guide to get to the video editor in YouTube Studio.
  2. Select Cards: Click on the 'Cards' row in the editor.
  3. Choose Card Type: Click 'Add Card' and select from Video, Playlist, or Channel.
  4. Set the Timing: Drag the card along the timeline to the exact point where you want it to appear. This should be a moment where the card is relevant to what's being discussed.
  5. Customize Text: Add a custom message and teaser text to entice viewers to click.
  6. Save Your Work: After adding all desired cards, click the 'Save' button.

Advantages and Outcomes of Strategic Use

  • Increased Watch Time: By guiding viewers to another one of your videos, you keep them on your channel longer, which is a key metric for the YouTube algorithm.
  • Higher Viewer Retention: You prevent viewers from leaving YouTube or clicking on a competitor's video after yours is finished.
  • Subscriber Growth: A prominent 'Subscribe' button on your end screen is a simple but highly effective way to convert viewers into subscribers.
  • Playlist Promotion: Funneling viewers into a playlist can lead to binge-watching sessions, dramatically increasing your channel's total views and watch time.
  • Data-Driven Decisions: YouTube Analytics shows you the click-through rate (CTR) of your cards and end screens, allowing you to refine your strategy over time.
