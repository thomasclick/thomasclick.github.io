What Are Super Chat, Super Stickers, and Super Thanks?

These three features, collectively known as 'Supers,' allow viewers to purchase special items to stand out and connect with you.

Super Chat

Super Chat is a feature for livestreams and Premieres. Viewers can purchase a highlighted message in the live chat feed. The message stays pinned for a certain amount of time, depending on how much the viewer paid, making it highly visible to you and everyone else in the stream.

Super Stickers

Similar to Super Chat, Super Stickers are purchased during livestreams and Premieres. Instead of a text message, viewers can buy animated stickers that pop up in the live chat, adding a fun and visual way to show support.

Super Thanks

Super Thanks allows viewers to show appreciation on your regular, uploaded videos (not just livestreams). A viewer can purchase a fun, one-time animation on the video page and post a colorful, distinct comment in the comments section to highlight their contribution.

Step-by-Step Guide to Enabling Fan Funding

To use these features, your channel must be in the YouTube Partner Program (YPP). Once you are, enabling them is straightforward:

  1. Go to YouTube Studio: Log in to your YouTube account and navigate to YouTube Studio.
  2. Select Monetization: In the left-hand menu, click on the 'Monetization' tab (it may be labeled 'Earn').
  3. Find the 'Supers' Tab: Within the Monetization section, you'll see several tabs like 'Overview,' 'Watch page ads,' etc. Click on the 'Supers' tab.
  4. Get Started: Click the 'Get Started' button and follow the on-screen instructions. You'll need to agree to the Commerce Product Addendum.
  5. Turn On Each Feature: Once enabled, you'll see toggle switches for Super Chat, Super Stickers, and Super Thanks. You can turn each one on individually.

Advantages and Outcomes: Why Use 'Supers'?

Integrating these features into your content strategy offers significant benefits:

  • Direct Monetization: Create a direct revenue stream based on audience appreciation, separate from ad revenue.

  • Instant Feedback: Highlighted Super Chats can guide your livestream content in real-time, showing you what your audience is most excited about.
  • Valuable Creator-Fan Interactions: Taking a moment to acknowledge a Super Chat or Super Thanks makes a viewer's day and encourages others to participate.
